About¶

I re-implemented the below mental health screeners as I found others were not adequately private or secure. I kept them true to their source as much as possible. They are largely geared towards adults and are likely not suitable for children. Results are outputted in a consistent single-line format to be saved in your notes app of choice. Lastly all tests are the sole ownership of their respective authors noted on each linked page.

Policy:

  • 100% client side
  • zero of the following: accounts, cookies, ads, trackers, referrals, unnecessary scripts, network calls
  • Tor Browser is explicitly exempted and encouraged
  • FOSS & contributions welcomed - source code (AGPL-3.0) available via: Codeberg, GitHub, GitLab

Disclaimer #1: These are not suitable for diagnosis, but are useful to determine if it is worth pursuing a professional opinion/assessment/treatment. Some can also be used to track your progression over time.

Disclaimer #2: I am not a mental health professional, just someone who has always found it interesting.

If you are currently in crisis please see the following resource list from HelpGuide.org.

Status: Twenty-two tests are currently available, with ~five more planned.
