Toronto Alexithymia Scale (TAS)

  • Summary from Wikipedia: The Toronto Alexithymia Scale (TAS) is the most widely used measure of alexithymia, a personality trait characterised by the difficulty in identifying and describing emotions. The scale provides a standardised, self-report method for assessing emotional awareness and has been used extensively in psychology research and clinical practice.
  • Author: Bagby, R. M., Parker, J. D., & Taylor, G. J. (1994).
