Generalized Anxiety Disorder-7 (GAD-7)

  • Summary from Wikipedia: The Generalized Anxiety Disorder 7-item scale (GAD-7) is a widely used self-administered diagnostic tool designed to screen for and assess the severity of generalized anxiety disorder (GAD).
  • Author: Drs. Robert L. Spitzer, Janet B.W. Williams, Kurt Kroenke, and colleagues

Questions¶

Over the last 2 weeks, how often have you been bothered by the following problems?

1. Feeling nervous, anxious or on edge



2. Not being able to stop or control worrying



3. Worrying too much about different things



4. Trouble relaxing



5. Being so restless that it is hard to sit still



6. Becoming easily annoyed or irritable



7. Feeling afraid as if something awful might happen



Results¶

Please complete the above questions and click the `Score` button to process results.

The possible severities are: none (0+), mild (5+), moderate (10+), severe (15+).
The max possible score is 21. A higher score is worse.
